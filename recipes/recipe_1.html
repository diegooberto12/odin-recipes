Eggs Benedict Casserole

Overnight Eggs Benedict! Easy to make ahead, perfect for brunch or a special occasion or overnight house guests.

ingredients

  1. cooking spray
  2. 8 large eggs
  3. 2 cups milk
  4. 3 green onions, chopped
  5. 1 teaspoon onion powder
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
  7. ¾ pound Canadian bacon, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  8. 6 English muffins, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  9. ½ teaspoon paprika
  10. 1 (.9 ounce) package hollandaise sauce mix
  11. 1 cup milk
  12. ¼ cup margarine
