Simple Southwestern Breakfast Pizza

This breakfast pizza is done in under 30 minutes and would be perfect for an addition to brunch

ingredients

  1. 3 pork sausage patties
  2. 1 large flour tortilla
  3. ½ cup shredded asadero cheese, divided
  4. ⅓ cup salsa
  5. 1 medium egg
  6. 4 slices whole-wheat rotini or farfalle pasta
  7. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
  8. 1 tablespoon crema con sal (salted Mexican-style sour cream)
  9. 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper to taste
